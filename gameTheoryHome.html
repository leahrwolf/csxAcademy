What is Game Theory?

Game Theory is a branch of mathematics that deals with strategic decision making among a set of participants. Game Theory deals with the moves or actions that each player makes.

external media

Key Terms:

  • Rationality: Everyone maximizes payoffs.
  • Pay-off: The outcome of a game.
  • Pay-off Matrix: The outcome of a game of different strategies shown in a table.
  • Value of Game: The expected outcome per play, when the players follow their optimal strategy.
