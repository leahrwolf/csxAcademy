What is P=NP?

P=NP is a major unsolved problem in computer science. It asks if all problems whose solution could be quickly found by a computer can also be quickly solved by a computer.

So what is P versus NP?

While P versus NP is a major unsolved problem in computer science that asks whether every problem whose solution can be quickly verified by a computer can also be quickly solved a computer. It was first mentioned by Kurt Gobel, the man on the left, who believed he had solved the problem. However, unfortunately he was not correct.
