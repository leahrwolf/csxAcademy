The picture of the left shows a great real life example where two rational thinking sides can etither choose to back down or hold ground for their respective sides. If the USA backs down and the USSR holds ground then the payoff is 50,-50 in favor of the USSR and if the USA holds ground while the USSR backs down, then the payoff is 50,-50 in favor of the USA.
